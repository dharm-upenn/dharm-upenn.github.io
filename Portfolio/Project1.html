"J.A.R.V.I.S, you up?" asked Tony;

"For you, sir, always," remarked JARVIS.

Flashback to the summer of 2008 – caramel popcorn in hand, captivated by the awe-inspiring tech world of Iron Man. Witnessing Tony Stark's bond with JARVIS sparked something in me – it wasn't just about code; it was about creating a digital companion, something personal yet immensely powerful. That moment ignited my fascination with technology, setting me on a journey that’s been fueled by curiosity ever since.

Following Frank Chimero’s mantra, ‘People ignore design that ignores people,’ I embarked on a path crafting interfaces that harmonize tech and human interaction seamlessly. My passion for product design stems from this ethos, striving to create interfaces that not only meet user needs but also inspire and connect on a deeper level.

So here I am, leveraging my tech-savvy roots and boundless curiosity to craft user-centric interfaces that resonate, blend innovation with purpose, and breathe life into every interaction. Join me as I continue this thrilling quest to merge human-centric design with the boundless possibilities of technology.
